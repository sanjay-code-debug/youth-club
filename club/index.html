Gopabandhu Youth Club History

Since 2015, Welcome to Gopabandhu Youth Club! Established in February 2024, we celebrate village events like cricket and volleyball. Founded in 2015, our club fosters unity and community engagement. Join us for sports, cultural exchange, and empowering initiatives. Together, let's build a brighter future.
